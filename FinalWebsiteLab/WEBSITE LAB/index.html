How we started

Back in 2017 CafeCoffee was created by Aaron Samuels, with his aspiration to create a safe space and a place of work. Aaron had been working at cafes throughout his summer and decided it was time to create his own special place. In 2017 Aaron Samuels started his cafe and officially owned his own store in downtown Calgary in 2021. Now CafeCoffee has grown and expanded their cafe to a location in Vancouver and Toronto. Here at CafeCoffee we value natural expensive ingredients to bring the best experience to you. Come visit us at one of our locations!
